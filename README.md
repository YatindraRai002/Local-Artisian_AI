## üåü Project Vision

India's traditional crafts represent centuries of cultural heritage, passed down through generations. However, many skilled artisans remain undiscovered, struggling to reach broader markets. Kala-Kaart aims to:

- **Preserve Cultural Heritage**: Document and showcase India's diverse traditional crafts
- **Empower Artisans**: Provide a digital platform for artisans to reach customers globally
- **Bridge Language Barriers**: Offer multilingual support for inclusive access
- **Data-Driven Discovery**: Use AI to match customers with the right artisans
- **Sustainable Livelihoods**: Create economic opportunities for rural craftspeople
